About

My name is Cliff Kurniawan. I am an Indonesian who is currently living in Sydney, Australia.

I was born in Jakarta in 1996, where i mostly spent my school life.

My experience with photography starts at an early age, as my father is a photographer himself in my church community in Jakarta. As soon as he lent me his camera for the first time, i knew right away that I'm going to spend a lot of time into this interesting tool.

My goal in photography is to share. To share my passion, my knowledge, my happiness. I am always challenged to take good photos that would bring joy to those who look at my work, as making people happy through what i love doing the most makes me happy as well.

You might not know me well just from this bio i wrote, but it is going to be an honor for me to capture your precious moments, where pictures are not just a bunch of JPEG and RAW files, but also happiness, to share.
